## Full description (4000 chars)

HydroFlow takes the guesswork out of staying hydrated. Your body is unique — your hydration plan should be too.

**PERSONALIZED DAILY GOALS**
Tell HydroFlow about yourself (weight, height, age, activity level, climate) and it calculates a custom daily water target. Goals adjust based on your local weather — hotter days mean more water.

**EASY TRACKING**
Log water, milk, juice, sports drinks, or any beverage in seconds. Create custom drinks for anything you regularly consume. Track by cup, bottle, or exact ml.

**AI BOTTLE SCANNER (PRO)**
Snap a photo of your water bottle and HydroFlow's AI instantly detects its capacity and current water level. No manual measuring needed.

**WEATHER-BASED DRINK ADVICE**
Get smart drink suggestions based on your local weather — cool refreshments for hot days, warm beverages when it's cold.

**HISTORY & INSIGHTS**
View 7-day hydration charts, daily breakdowns, streaks, and milestones. Track your progress and stay motivated with achievements.

**SMART REMINDERS**
Set customizable reminders throughout the day. Never forget to drink water again.

**EXPORT YOUR DATA**
Export all your hydration logs as CSV to analyze your own way.

**YOUR DATA STAYS PRIVATE**
No account needed. All health data stays on your device. No login, no signup, no tracking.

**PRO FEATURES**
- AI Bottle Scanner (photo-based capacity & fill detection)
- Unlimited custom beverages
- Ad-free experience
- Advanced insights
